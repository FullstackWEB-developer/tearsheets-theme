The Portformer Replacement Score is the average of four metrics:

  • Consistent The extent to which each weighted ETF positions aligns to {{ .Params.meta.ticker }} over time and through different regimes.
  • Complete The extent to which each weighted ETF positions correlates to {{ .Params.meta.ticker }} in the short term.
  • Simplicity This metric is closely related to the Herfindahl Hirschman Index (HHI) and is a measure of the average concentration of the replication weights of each weighted ETF positions over time as it tracks {{ .Params.meta.ticker }}.
  • Confidence If above 50, this suggests a greater statitically likelyhood of the outperformance of the benchmark over {{ .Params.meta.ticker }} if historical patterns continue.
